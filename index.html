Time off accruals

Time Off in Lieu (TOIL) is a form of paid time off earned through additional hours worked in place of overtime pay.

Allow employees to take Time off in Lieu (TOIL)
ON
1.5 x per hour worked — Time off in Lieu ⌄
This rule applies when employees work additional hours beyond their standard schedule. For every hour of overtime worked, employees earn 1.5 hours of time off in lieu. This time off can be used for future paid leave requests.
1.75 x per hour worked — Time off in Lieu ⌄
This enhanced rate applies for overtime worked during weekends, holidays, or particularly demanding periods. For every hour worked under these conditions, employees earn 1.75 hours of time off in lieu.
